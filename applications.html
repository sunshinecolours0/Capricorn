Where our materials are used

Built for harsh environments and temperature extremes

Our accessory products support insulation systems by sealing joints, blocking vapor ingress, resisting corrosion under insulation (CUI), and protecting the insulation layer from weather, chemicals, vibration, and thermal cycling.

⛽

Oil & Gas Refineries

Applied on insulated pipelines, columns and vessels to prevent CUI and retain thermal efficiency in high-temperature zones.

🧬

Petrochemical Complexes

Designed for chemically aggressive and high-humidity environments—tight sealing and long-term performance on process equipment.

❄️

LNG & Cryogenic

Ultra-low temperature applications to prevent vapor ingress; specialized cryogenic mastics and vapor barriers for mission-critical systems.

⚡

Power Plants

Support ductwork, turbines, steam pipelines and boilers—reducing thermal loss and protecting insulation layers.

🧪

Fertilizer & Chemical

Chemical resistance and moisture protection for insulation exposed to fluctuating temperatures and corrosive fumes.

🏢

Building & HVAC

Chilled water lines, ducts, roofs and acoustic enclosures—improving thermal efficiency and moisture resistance.

🌊

Offshore & Marine

Formulated to endure saline air, vibration and extreme weather—helping insulation systems remain intact.

🏭

Process Industries

Seal insulation around dryers, kilns, tanks and rotary equipment—reducing heat loss and maintaining process stability.
