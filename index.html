What we make

A focused portfolio for industrial insulation integrity—bonding, sealing, weatherproofing, vapor control and long-life protection.

Mastics & Coatings

Vapour barrier mastics, weather barrier mastics, aluminium finish mastics, anti-abrasion and protective coatings.

Adhesives
